Demo details

✓Mobile gestures. Tap to flip, pinch to zoom, and one-finger rotate work without tiny controls.
✓Safe/bleed review. Toggle guides before sending card art to print or changing the trim.
✓Matched pairs. The selector swaps each tactic front and historical case-study back together.
✓Physical mockup. Rounded corners, beveled edge, thickness, glare, and floor shadow make it feel like a real card.
✓Performance. The app uses external WebP card faces for mobile load times and keeps PNG production files in the repo.
